# Style Guide

Status: Seed working guide for repo outputs. This does not replace Stripe corporate guidance.

## Writing standards

- Prefer concrete nouns and verbs.
- Keep paragraphs short.
- Use one sharp number when it adds credibility.
- Lead with the real-world implication, not the internal framing process.
- Avoid generic corporate superlatives.

## Claims standard

Every non-obvious claim must be either:
- sourceable now, or
- marked `UNVERIFIED`
