# Crisis Playbooks

Status: Seed scaffold.

## Core doctrine

In a crisis, clarity beats completeness, and disciplined uncertainty beats fast speculation.

## Shared first-hour checklist

1. What is confirmed?
2. What is unknown?
3. Who already knows?
4. Who might publish before we respond?
5. Does SF or another region have activity that could create friendly fire?
6. What do employees need to know before they hear it elsewhere?
