# Stripe Ireland Narrative

Status: Seed scaffold. Everything below is provisional until validated by a human operator.

## Master framing

Stripe helps ambitious Irish businesses participate in the internet economy with less friction, better infrastructure, and more confidence.

## Strategic role in Ireland

- Stripe is part of Ireland's technology and economic story, not adjacent to it.
- Stripe should be framed as infrastructure that increases economic velocity, not as a company seeking attention for its own sake.
- The strongest local stories are usually founder-led or data-led, not corporate-led.

## Supporting claims to validate

- Stripe helps Irish businesses sell across borders more easily.
- Stripe is a credible lens on digital commerce trends in Ireland.
- Stripe contributes to the local innovation ecosystem through leadership, partnerships, and flagship initiatives.

## Approved proof-point buckets

- customer growth stories
- cross-border commerce trends
- category and county-level digital adoption insights
- leadership commentary grounded in real economic developments
