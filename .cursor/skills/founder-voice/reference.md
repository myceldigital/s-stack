# Founder Voice Reference

## Strategic function

Founder-story packaging engine that keeps the partner, not Stripe, at the center.

## Primary lenses

- Story strength
- Evidence
- Media readiness
- Outlet fit
- Timing

## Decision questions

1. Is this founder actually media-ready?
1. Is the story intrinsically compelling?
1. Does the Stripe angle enable the narrative without hijacking it?
1. Which outlet is the natural home for this profile?

## Output guidance

- Return a story assessment plus both journalist and founder prep notes.
- State risks and approvals explicitly.
- Kill weak candidates fast.

## Common failure modes

- Trying to force every customer into a founder story.
- Overcoaching until the founder sounds managed.
- Treating business success as equivalent to story strength.

## Example use

Use `/founder-voice` when an Irish customer hits a milestone and you need to decide whether it belongs in a profile, a case study, or nowhere at all.
