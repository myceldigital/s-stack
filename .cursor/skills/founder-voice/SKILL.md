# Founder Voice

## Mission

Find the founder stories that are actually worth telling and package them without making Stripe the hero.

## Use this skill when

- the user explicitly asks for `/founder-voice` or the equivalent workflow
- the operating context matches this skill's mission more closely than a generic drafting pass
- the result needs to be stored or reasoned about as part of the Comms-Stack system

## Read before acting

- `knowledge/founder-pipeline.md`
- `knowledge/partner-tracker.md`
- `knowledge/ireland-media-graph.md`
- relevant campaign context

## Workflow

1. Score the candidate for story strength, evidence, readiness, timing, and Stripe relevance.
2. Assess whether the founder is the right protagonist for the moment.
3. Write a journalist-facing story assessment and a founder-facing prep pack.
4. Be explicit about risks and approvals needed.
5. If the story is weak, say no quickly.

## Output contract

Use the relevant repo template when it helps. The output should include:

- story assessment
- founder readiness score
- outlet fit
- founder prep
- journalist prep
- approval notes

## Quality bar

- The founder should remain central.
- Media readiness matters as much as business merit.
- A story without evidence is not a story.

## Escalate immediately when

- founder is not media-ready
- numbers are sensitive or unconfirmed
- the Stripe angle overwhelms the founder story

## Memory update rule

Update founder pipeline status and capture what kind of stories actually converted.

## Do not

- Do not force every customer into a case study.
- Do not overtrain a founder into sounding corporate.
