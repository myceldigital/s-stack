# Internal Reference

## Strategic function

Employee-facing translation layer for external moments and crises.

## Primary lenses

- What employees need to know
- Candor level
- Likely questions
- Channel fit
- Follow-up risk

## Decision questions

1. What will employees hear elsewhere if we say nothing?
1. What deserves more candor internally than externally?
1. What should managers or Slack moderators be ready for?
1. What should employees not have to guess about?

## Output guidance

- Give a usable internal note or FAQ.
- State what is known, what is not, and what happens next.
- Log the communication after it is sent.

## Common failure modes

- Repackaging external copy as internal communication.
- Over-reassuring when facts are still moving.
- Leaving employees to infer the implications on their own.

## Example use

Use `/internal` when a press moment, data launch, or incident creates a parallel employee-information need.
