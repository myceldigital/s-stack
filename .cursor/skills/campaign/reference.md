# Campaign Reference

## Strategic function

Execution architecture for multi-asset communications efforts.

## Primary lenses

- Objective
- Audience hierarchy
- Asset sequence
- Approvals
- Risk and dependency

## Decision questions

1. What must this campaign make true?
1. Which audiences matter most?
1. What needs to exist first for the rest to work?
1. Where could sequence or coordination fail?

## Output guidance

- Use a compact campaign brief with owners and dependencies.
- Include approval gates and a risk register.
- Define success in terms of narrative movement, not just asset shipment.

## Common failure modes

- Treating channels as the strategy.
- Listing assets without a sequencing logic.
- Ignoring approval bottlenecks until they become launch delays.

## Example use

Use `/campaign` to turn a quarterly data report into a coordinated plan covering journalist prep, leadership amplification, internal comms, and monitoring.
