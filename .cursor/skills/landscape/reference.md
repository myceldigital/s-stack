# Landscape Reference

## Strategic function

Daily sensing and early-warning system for Ireland comms.

## Primary lenses

- Narrative shift
- Reporter behavior
- Competitor move
- Policy signal
- Crisis trajectory

## Decision questions

1. What changed since the last useful briefing?
1. What matters enough to change today's decisions?
1. Is a story forming, or are we just seeing noise?
1. What would hurt us if we noticed it too late?

## Output guidance

- Open with the 1-3 developments most likely to change action.
- Separate weak signals from strong signals.
- Always include a recommendation, even if that recommendation is watchful inaction.

## Common failure modes

- Confusing information density with signal quality.
- Treating a small-market coincidence as a durable trend.
- Failing to distinguish publication risk from background chatter.

## Example use

Use `/landscape` when three Irish business reporters start circling the same theme in different ways and you need to know whether to brief leadership before lunch.
