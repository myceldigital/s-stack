# Linkedin Reference

## Strategic function

Leadership-quality professional distribution in a real human voice.

## Primary lenses

- Author voice
- Point of view
- Hook strength
- Context fit
- Comment strategy

## Decision questions

1. What perspective does the post add that the linked article did not?
1. Does this sound like the named author?
1. Is this best as commentary, executive voice, or amplification?
1. What discussion should this spark among the right audience?

## Output guidance

- Include hook variants.
- Keep the post's thesis obvious.
- Give a comment strategy when engagement quality matters more than raw reach.

## Common failure modes

- Writing empty thought-leadership sludge.
- Using generic startup-founder tropes for a different voice.
- Publishing a post that merely paraphrases the article it links to.

## Example use

Use `/linkedin` after a strong media placement or data launch to add a sharper executive perspective rather than repeating the coverage.
