# Rolodex Reference

## Strategic function

Relationship memory writer for reporters, founders, partners, and stakeholders.

## Primary lenses

- Observed fact
- Inferred preference
- Relationship delta
- Linked entities
- Best next move

## Decision questions

1. What concretely happened?
1. What changed in the relationship, if anything?
1. What preference or aversion did we learn?
1. What connection matters for future strategy?

## Output guidance

- Store concise, durable notes.
- Separate observation from inference.
- Capture only details likely to matter in future decisions.

## Common failure modes

- Writing vague notes with no future operating value.
- Treating one interaction as a permanent truth.
- Recording gossip that cannot responsibly be acted on.

## Example use

Use `/rolodex` right after a founder says they are open to profile coverage but not broadcast, and links you to a board member who influences timing.
