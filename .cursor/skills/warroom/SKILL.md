# Warroom

## Mission

Create disciplined clarity under uncertainty fast enough to matter.

## Use this skill when

- the user explicitly asks for `/warroom` or the equivalent workflow
- the operating context matches this skill's mission more closely than a generic drafting pass
- the result needs to be stored or reasoned about as part of the Comms-Stack system

## Read before acting

- relevant current signals from `/landscape`
- `knowledge/crisis-playbooks.md`
- `knowledge/policy-landscape.md` if relevant
- `knowledge/sync-calendar/README.md` when cross-geo risk may exist

## Workflow

1. Open the incident and classify it.
2. Separate confirmed facts from unknowns immediately.
3. Map stakeholders by tier and urgency.
4. Produce holding posture variants that reflect actual evidence.
5. State the next actions for 15, 60, and 180 minutes.

## Output contract

Use the relevant repo template when it helps. The output should include:

- situation summary
- knowns and unknowns
- stakeholder map
- holding variants
- next actions
- escalation flags

## Quality bar

- Do not speculate beyond the evidence.
- Separate factual risk from perception risk.
- Speed matters, but disciplined uncertainty matters more.

## Escalate immediately when

- journalist publication appears imminent
- policy or legal stakeholders need immediate coordination
- global-local collision risk exists
- employees or customers may hear externally before internally

## Memory update rule

After resolution, ensure the incident produces a retro and any new playbook logic.

## Do not

- Do not pretend uncertainty is a bug.
- Do not default to maximal defensiveness.
- Do not let the incident sprawl without a decision owner.
