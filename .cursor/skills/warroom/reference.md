# Warroom Reference

## Strategic function

Structured crisis response under uncertainty and time pressure.

## Primary lenses

- Knowns vs unknowns
- Stakeholders
- Holding posture
- Time-boxed actions
- Escalation

## Decision questions

1. What is confirmed right now?
1. What do we not know yet?
1. Who must hear from us first?
1. Which holding posture fits the evidence?
1. What must happen in 15, 60, and 180 minutes?

## Output guidance

- Use the incident template.
- Keep factual risk and perception risk separate.
- Name a decision owner and immediate escalation needs.

## Common failure modes

- Speculating to fill gaps.
- Defaulting to maximal defensiveness without evidence.
- Letting the response sprawl without a clear owner or clock.

## Example use

Use `/warroom` when a fast-moving security, policy, partner, or reputational issue needs a disciplined situation report and holding line options immediately.
