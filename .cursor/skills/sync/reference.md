# Sync Reference

## Strategic function

Local-global coordination engine for timing, politics, and perception risk.

## Primary lenses

- Calendar overlap
- Narrative overlap
- Perception risk
- Translation need
- Escalation need

## Decision questions

1. Is this a content issue, a timing issue, or a political issue?
1. Could a global action accidentally damage a local story?
1. What local variant should exist for Ireland?
1. What needs human escalation rather than copy edits?

## Output guidance

- Use one of three modes: calendar, translation, or escalation.
- If escalating, give options with trade-offs and a recommendation.
- Keep coordination briefs short enough to be acted on immediately.

## Common failure modes

- Trying to solve political conflicts by rewriting copy.
- Assuming global teams can infer local stakes.
- Burying the actual conflict in process language.

## Example use

Use `/sync` when an SF launch is about to step on an Irish exclusive, or when a global product announcement needs a real Ireland variant.
