# Policy Reference

## Strategic function

Comms-relevant interpretation of Irish and EU policy developments.

## Primary lenses

- Immediate impact
- Near-term narrative effect
- Stakeholder relevance
- Coordination need
- Uncertainty

## Decision questions

1. Why does this policy development matter to communications?
1. Who needs to be looped in?
1. What can we say confidently now?
1. What remains uncertain or contested?

## Output guidance

- Return a comms-facing policy brief, not a legal memo.
- Classify the issue as immediate, near-term, or structural.
- Name the recommended coordination path.

## Common failure modes

- Sounding like legal counsel without the basis to do so.
- Flooding the operator with low-value policy noise.
- Forgetting that policy significance is not identical to media significance.

## Example use

Use `/policy` when DPC, CBI, Budget Day, or EU digital regulation developments are likely to shift how Stripe is discussed in Ireland.
